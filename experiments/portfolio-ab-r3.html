Option B Fish-eye toward today — recent ships bigger, archive small.

A recency-weighted manifest. The most recent ship gets a hero entry (bigger title, real summary, callout results). Recent ships get mid-sized entries. Older ships compress to one-line strips. The same fish-eye logic from the obsidian Score, applied to the work.

Strength: a peer/recruiter lands on the work that's still warm; the page has a felt arc forward. Weakness: the system enforces "recent = important"; a 2022 paper with lasting impact is one line.
