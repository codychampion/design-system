Option B Subtraction pass — three small redundancies removed.

Five things gone, the rhythm changes audibly. Brand meta gone. Eyebrow gone. The housekeeping paragraph gone — it restated the lede and moved availability that the postscript carries. Year strips shrink to a tick — a small mono label, hairline rule, no count, no "what." The pull-quote becomes the body. The page is shorter; the manifest sits up.

Strength: the difference is visible at a glance, not in tooltip diffs. The page enacts its own principle: less furniture, each remaining element earns its row. Weakness: the year strip is now nearly invisible — a reader scanning for "what year is this" works harder. The per-row date still answers it, but the section break is quieter.
