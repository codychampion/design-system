§ 01 — Foundations

Three modes, one author.

Same hand, different registers. Modes are not themes; they are physical references that decide the shape of every choice that follows.

A mode is the room the work is being read in. Pick the room before you build the furniture.

Modes are mutually exclusive per surface. If a page wants two modes, it wants to be two pages. Hard rule.

§ 06 — Foundations

Motion — slower than reflex.

Stillness is the default; motion is the signal. One thing at a time, with one easing curve.

TokenValueUse
--easingcubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)The system curve — never deviate
--dur-micro200msHover, focus, micro-state
--dur-comp350msComponent transitions
--dur-page600msPage-level moves

Demonstration

600ms · cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)
Hard nos: bounce, elastic, decorative looping, layout-shift on hover, scroll-jacking, universal "reveal on scroll."

Per mode: obsidian rare and operational · signal supports reading flow · field permits subtle hero parallax <50px.

§ 08 — Identity

The crest — Tier 1, universal.

Hexagonal frame, fox, trident, crystalline staff. One asset, recoloured by mode. Heritage as structure.

The Champion family crest is the singular personal mark across all modes. Iconographic load: hexagonal frame for molecular structure (PhD heritage), fox head for hunter intelligence, trident-with-arrows for the literal surname, crystalline staff for scholarship.

Render via PNG mask + background-color for per-mode tinting, so a single asset serves every surface.
