— Plates · the surfaces in three modes

Three surfaces, three modes, each in light and dark, at desktop and phone.

One canonical surface per mode, rendered in scaled iframes at both light and dark themes. The previews are the real pages at their real viewport widths — each iframe loads with ?theme=light|dark so the responsive behavior and the palette logic are both what you'd see on a real device. Click any preview to open it full-size in its theme.

Plate I

The Manifest — reverse-chronological work, in plain prose.

signal

Brand strip stripped to a wordmark, a sentence lede that does the work of a tagline, a flat year-grouped manifest under past wash (2025 inked, 2024 muted, 2023+ caption-quiet, hover restores), an editorial peek on each row (italic Lora, hidden until hover, always-visible on touch), and an obsidian-mode postscript at the foot carrying live status. Settled across A/B rounds R1–R8.

Plate II

The Briefing — time-as-spine, score-shaped, past wash.

obsidian

A morning briefing built like a musical score. An opening sentence over a counts ribbon, tiered staves for calendar (primary), tasks & systems (secondary), reading & weather (tertiary). Day-zones (overnight · morning · midday · afternoon · after) hold the horizontal axis stable. Stems above a hairline mark — type breathes, the bar is restrained.

Plate III

The Journal — long-form, marginalia, paper grain.

field

A naturalist’s field journal entry: italic Newsreader title, two-column body with marginalia in the gutter, gold accent reserved for pull-quotes and section marks, subtle paper-grain texture. Long enough that the reader settles in; quiet enough that the type does the work. Tufte’s Beautiful Evidence shelved next to a paper notebook.
